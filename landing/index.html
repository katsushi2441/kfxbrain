TradingAgents

The full multi-agent graph, unpatched: analyst reports → bull/bear debate → trader plan → risk debate → final decision, on real market data. Measured ~5.5 minutes of compute per run — priced accordingly at $0.50. Everything else is $0.05.

FinGPT

ai-hedge-fund

13 investor personas (Buffett, Munger, Burry…) via the upstream prompt builders with only the LLM transport swapped. Honest by design: Buffett declines FX as outside his circle of competence. Plus news sentiment and portfolio synthesis.

Apache-2.0 · NEW

FinRobot

The Market Forecaster workflow (2-4 positive developments and concerns, next-week move with a % range) plus 8 analyst-report instructions — income statement, balance sheet, cash flow, segments, risk, competitors, highlights, company description — extracted from the pinned source at runtime.

MIT · NEW

FinMem

Layered-memory decisions: pass short/mid/long/reflection memories and the agent decides — switching between a risk-seeking and risk-averse character based on your cumulative return, exactly as the paper designed. A reflection endpoint turns outcomes into storable lessons.

ORIGINAL · NEW

Market intelligence

Not vendored — kfxbrain originals: rank up to 40 pairs in one call (risk-adjusted opportunity or currency flow), cross-pair anomaly detection, margin-call / stop-out risk ranking, and a single-pair evidence-bounded signal. Uses only the evidence you supply.

Try before you pay — two ways

We built verification in, because "trust me" is not a sales pitch.

1. The free browser Workbench

The FX Intelligence Workbench is a live console against the same API: pick an endpoint, load a EUR/JPY preset or paste your own evidence JSON, and read the structured judgment — with a health indicator showing the Gemma backend is actually up. Judge the quality yourself before spending a cent.

2. Verify the x402 machinery without paying

Every paid endpoint answers unpaid requests with a signed 402 challenge — so you can confirm the exact price, wallet, and network before any money moves:

curl -X POST https://bittensorman.xyz/fxbrain/finmem/decide
# → HTTP 402: {"maxAmountRequired":"50000", "payTo":"0x444f…", "network":"base", …}

The full catalog is machine-readable at https://bittensorman.xyz/.well-known/x402.json — 36 paths under /fxbrain/. JPYC rail: https://exbridge.ddns.net:18327/fxbrain/… (7.5 JPYC per call, 75 JPYC for the full graph). Also on Bankr.
